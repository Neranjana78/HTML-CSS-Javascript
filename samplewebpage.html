ABOUT SASTRA

SASTRA (Shanmugha Arts, Science, Technology & Research Academy) University, located in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India, is a renowned institution known for its academic excellence, innovative research, and comprehensive educational programs. Established in 1984, SASTRA has grown to become a prominent player in the fields of engineering, science, management, law, and humanities. The university offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, attracting students from across the country and abroad. SASTRA is committed to fostering a stimulating learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a strong emphasis on research and development. The institution also places a high value on industry collaboration and practical exposure, ensuring that its graduates are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the global workforce.
