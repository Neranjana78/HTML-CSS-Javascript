ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY

SASTRA (Shanmugha Arts, Science, Technology & Research Academy) University, located in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India, is a renowned institution known for its academic excellence, innovative research, and comprehensive educational programs. Established in 1984, SASTRA has grown to become a prominent player in the fields of engineering, science, management, law, and humanities. SASTRA is committed to fostering a stimulating learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a strong emphasis on research and development.

INSIDE THE CAMPUS
PROGRAMS :
SASTRA offers a wide range of programs including undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degrees in fields such as engineering, science, management, law, humanities, and education.
FACULTY :
The university boasts highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a culture of research and innovation.
RESEARCH:
SASTRA places a strong emphasis on research and development, with numerous research centers and labs focusing on various disciplines. The university encourages faculty and students to engage in cutting-edge research projects.
